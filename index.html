About Me






I'm Evon, a UX designer and psychology student at the University of Waterloo. Art and design have always been an interest of mine. I've had the pleasure of working as a UX designer for companies like Descarte Systems Group and Triyosoft in the past. In my free time, I enjoy travelling and spending time with my grandma.
